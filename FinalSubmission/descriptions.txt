Title: Keep it lit!

Short description: Try to keep your fire lit and escape from the island in this fun survival game!

Long description: Keep it lit is a survival game in which you are a stranded sailor who needs to escape. As luck would have it, a lightning
made a fire for you, which can be your only hope for surivival. Try to keep it lit by gathering wood or fuel around the island, as it is
vital for your escape. Not only wood, but also stone and ores can be gathered around the island. Using these resources, you can start building
your escape raft. Be cautious though, as there are animals around, and not all are friendly...
